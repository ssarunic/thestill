Two of today's interviews land on the same fault line. On Lenny's Podcast, Zevi Arnovitz — a non-technical PM at Meta — argued the bar has fundamentally shifted for new graduates entering tech.

> It's the best time to be a junior, contrary to what a lot of people are saying — when else in history could you get out of school and just build a startup on your own?

> The scaling laws didn't bend, they just moved — and the people who already knew how to ship are pulling away from the ones who are still learning.
